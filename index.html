January Brings us firefox 85

To wrap up January, we are proud to bring you the release of Firefox 85, Is this version we are bringing you support for the :focus visible pseudo-class in CSS and associated devtools, and the complete removal of Flash support from Firefox.

Apple Silicon-based

The release of Macs at the end of last year generated a flurry of news coverage and some surprises at the machine's performarce. This post details some backgrouns information on the experience of porting Firefox to run natively on these CPUs.

We'll start with some background on the Mac transition and give an overview of Firefox internals that needed to know about the new architecture, before moving on to the concept of Universal Binaries.

We'll then explain how DRM/EME works on the new platform, talk about our experience with macOs Big Sur, and discuss various updater problems we have to deal with. We'll conclude with the release ans an overview of various other improviments that are in the pipeline.
